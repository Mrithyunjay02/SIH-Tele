Simple & Secure Health Access

Our registration is designed for everyone. You can sign up yourself, or a family member or local ASHA worker can help you.

Information You'll Need

  • Mobile Number (Verified with OTP)
  • Full Name, Age, and Gender
  • Village/Town (Select from a simple list)
  • A simple 4-Digit PIN for login

Optional (Add Later)

  • Aadhaar number for a health ID
  • Link family members (children & elders)

Special Features

  • Works Offline: Access records without internet
  • Assisted Registration: Get help from an ASHA worker
